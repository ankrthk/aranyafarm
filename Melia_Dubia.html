Introduction

Basics About Melia Dubia

  • Melia Dubia is found in many region all around the world. Basically it is crop of Malaysia.
  • In India mainly it is grown in south India. But now many states have adopted this crop due to shortage of wood in plywood industry, irrigation and labour problems, fluctuation in rates of routine crops, negative approach of new generation for agriculture & good return of Melia Dubia.
  • Plywood industry is supplied wood by forestry & import in India. But due to the limitations of above sources, future of Melia Dubia is very bright.
  • Melia Dubia can be planted in the atmosphere between 20 c. to 45 c. It gets faster growth by water of 500 tds to 2000 tds. It can be grown in all lands but whirlpool land is very much suitable for this crop.
  • It takes 7-8 years for harvesting.

Investment In One Acre

  • 300 PLANTS CAN BE PLANTED IN ONE ACRE AT THE DISTANCE OF 12 X 12 FT. ONE PLANT COST 20 RS. SO, 300 X 20 = 6000 RS.
  • CHEMICAL FREE FERTILIZER OF 15 RS IS USED IN ONE PIT. SO, 300 X 15 = 4500 RS. [ FERTILIZER RATES MAY VARY ACCORDING TO LOCATION ]
  • DIGGING PITS & PLANTING THE PLANTS COST 6 TO 8 RS PER PLANT. SO, 300 X 8 = 2400 RS. [ THIS COST MAY VARY ACCORDING TO VAGES OF LABOURS OF DIFFERENT AREAS ]
  • ALL OTHER EXPENSES LIKE :- FENCING, LAND PREPARATION, BORE WELL, INSTALLATION OF DRIP & OTHERS WILL BE ADDITIONAL. [ THOSE EXPENSES WILL BE MENTIONED IN PROJECT REPORT ]

Profit In Melia Dubia

Economics Of Malabar Neem

  • In Gujarat, Maharashtra, & M.P price of Melia Dubia wood is 4000 to 6000 per ton, according to quality & location.
  • In south India, rate is 8000 to 10000 per ton. It is purchased in cft. also according to girth.
  • An adult tree of 8 years gives 800 to 1000 kg of wood or 15 to 18 cft. of wood.
  • We can plant 194 plants in one acre on the distance of 15*15 ft. One tree gives minimum 4000 rs. So 194 rs x 4000 rs =7,76,000 rs can be earned from one acre in 10 yrs.
  • Though we get 75% result, we can earn 5,84,000 from one acre in 10 yrs.
  • We can generate extra income by intercropping. Approximately we can earn 2,72,000 rs as extra income from one acre in 10 yrs.
  • So, profit of one acre after ten years can be 7,76,000 rs + 2,72,000 rs = 10,48,000 rs.
