How many acres should be cultivated for your service?

In Gujarat,minimum 10 Acres are required for our Consultancy services. And out of Gujarat, minimum 15 Acres are required for our services.

What is the rate of Melia Dubia Plants?

20 rs per Plant Loading Rate. Free Transportations on minimum 5000's Plants Order.

What are the rates of Malabar Neem Wood?

In SOUTH BHARAT, Plywood Industries give into 7000 Rs to 8000 Rs per ton for Malabar Neem Wood. In Gujarat, Maharastra, Rajasthan, M.P, U.P, they provide 6500 Rs per ton for the wood.

What is the weather & soil type Required for Melia Dubia Plantation?

Melia Dubia can be planted in the range of 15°C - 45°C. There is no Paricular type of Soil Required for it's Plantation. Only One requriment is Water should not stop into your Soil.
