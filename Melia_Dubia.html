Introduction And

Need To Know Before

  • MELIA DUBIA , ALSO KNOWN AS MALABAR NEEM, ROYAL TREE, MALASYIN NEEM ETC., IS A MELIACEAE FAMILY TREE. IT HAS BECOME FAMOUS ALL ACROSS THE BHARAT BECAUSE OF ITâ€™S FAST GROWING CAPACITY. IT IS A SOFTWOOD TIMBER WITH MULTIPURPOSE USES. IT CAN BE HARVESTED WITHIN 7 TO 8 YEARS. IT IS USEFUL FOR MANY INDUSTRIES, SUCH AS:- PAPERWOOD, FIREWOOD, FURNITURE, PLYWOOD & PANEL.
  • MINIMUM 20 TO 30 INCHES OF RAIN IS NECESSARY FOR BETTER GROWTH. RAIN WATER SHOULD NOT STOP INTO FARM FOR LONG TIME.
  • AT INITIAL STAGE IRRIGATION IS A KEY TO ACHIEVE BEST GROWTH. WE NEED TO WATER PLANTS UPTO 3 YEARS DEPENDING ON GROWTH. TDS VALUE OF WATER SHOULD NOT BE MORE THAN 1000 FOR BEST RESULTS.
  • PH VALUE OF LAND SHOULD BE BETWEEN 5.5 TO 8 FOR OPTIMUM GROWTH. HARVESTING CAN BE DONE IN 7 YEARS IN FERTILE & IRRIGATED LAND.
  • DISTANCE BETWEEN PLANTS SHOULD BE 12 X 12 FT. OR 15 X 15 FT. AT THE DISTANCE OF 12 X 12 FT. 304 PLANTS & AT THE DISTANCE OF 15 X 15 FT. 192 PLANTS CAN BE PLANTED IN ONE ACRE.
  • INTERCROPPING IS POSSIBLE UPTO 3 YEARS. NATURAL FARMING IS MUST FOR BETTER FUTURE OF SOIL, PLANTS & COUNTRY.

Expense In Malabar Neem Farming


Investment In One Acre

  • 300 PLANTS CAN BE PLANTED IN ONE ACRE AT THE DISTANCE OF 12 X 12 FT. ONE PLANT COST 20 RS. SO, 300 X 20 = 6000 RS.
  • CHEMICAL FREE FERTILIZER OF 15 RS IS USED IN ONE PIT. SO, 300 X 15 = 4500 RS. [ FERTILIZER RATES MAY VARY ACCORDING TO LOCATION ]
  • DIGGING PITS & PLANTING THE PLANTS COST 6 TO 8 RS PER PLANT. SO, 300 X 8 = 2400 RS. [ THIS COST MAY VARY ACCORDING TO VAGES OF LABOURS OF DIFFERENT AREAS ]
  • ALL OTHER EXPENSES LIKE :- FENCING, LAND PREPARATION, BORE WELL, INSTALLATION OF DRIP & OTHERS WILL BE ADDITIONAL. [ THOSE EXPENSES WILL BE MENTIONED IN PROJECT REPORT ]

Economics Of Malabar Neem Farming

Growth Required

  • WE NEED TO KNOW ONE THING VERY CLEARLY. TO MAKE MELIA DUBIA FARMING PROFITABLE,MINIMUM GIRTH OF TREE SHOULD BE 36 INCHES UPTO 20-25 FEET FROM THE BOTTOM. TIMBER IS CUT INTO LOGS OF 08 FEETS. ON BOTH THE END GIRTH SHOULD BE 36 INCHES TO BE MOST BENEFICIAL FOR COMPANY & FARMERS.

PROFIT

  • ONE TREE PRODUCE 750-1000 KG WEIGHT OR 15-20 CFT. OF WOOD.
  • IT COST 6000-8000 RS PER TON OR 325-450 RS PER CFT. IT MEANS ONE TREE CAN FETCH 4000-8000 RS AS PURE PROFIT.
  • IN 8 YEARS 12-15 RS LACS EARNING IS POSSIBLE FROM ONE ACRE.
  • ALL OTHER WOOD MATERIAL OF A TREE ALSO CAN BE SOLD TO OTHER INDUSTRIES.
